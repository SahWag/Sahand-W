I was born in the Netherlands on the 8th of December, 2000. In 2021, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at TU Delft. Afterwards, I started the Master's programme Robotics at TU Delft After completing my coursework, I took a year off to dedicate all my time to being a software and robotics engineer for Project MARCH, a student-led research project where we designed, built and programmed an fully motorized lower-body exoskeleton for a paraplegic pilot. Now, I'm finishing up my degree programme, I'm currently writing my thesis on Eigenmanifold control for underactuated robotic systems
I hope to add more to this page over time, as I'm interested in pursuing academia and furthering the field of robotic control! :)

Besides my academic career, I program and design video games in my spare time, and play music in my free time.
